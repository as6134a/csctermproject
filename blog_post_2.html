How We Differ from University Sponsored Tutoring

Matt Fields

November 16, 2018



Peer 4 Peer takes pride in its accessibility to AU students of any major. Here, we will discuss how our organization differs from tutoring that the university provides.

  • Tutoring available for all classes regardless of major
  • More flexible time schedule for both students and tutors
  • Tutors can choose when and how often they work
